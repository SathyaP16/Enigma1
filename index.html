ENIGMA 5.0
SOLVE. BUILD. SWING.

Every city has an alter ego, and every hackathon needs one too. Enigma 5.0 is SIES CSI's flagship hackathon — 36 hours in the dark, one mask to earn, and two open cases waiting to be cracked: FinTech and HealthTech. Suit up in teams of four and swing in.

The Brief

Two Open Cases,
One Night City

Enigma 5.0 is open to undergraduate students, competing in teams of exactly four. Pick a track, pick your alias, and start building before the clock runs out.

TRACK FILE // 01

FinTech

Build tools that move money smarter — fraud detection, micro-investing, payments infrastructure, or the next quiet revolution in banking. The vault is open; find the crack in the system.

PaymentsFraud DetectionInvestingOpen Banking
TRACK FILE // 02

HealthTech

Design solutions that keep people alive and well — diagnostics, care access, mental health, or hospital logistics. Every good vigilante story starts with someone worth saving.

DiagnosticsCare AccessMental HealthMed-Logistics
📌
ELIGIBILITY — Open to undergraduate students only. Teams must have exactly 4 members. Cross-college teams are welcome; every member needs a valid ID and a working alias. One registration per team.
The Countdown

Timeline

Mark your calendar. The mask doesn't wait for stragglers.

Phase 01 — Registrations Open

Teams Assemble

Squads of four lock in their track, choose an alias, and submit their registration.

Phase 02 — Registrations Close

The Files Seal Shut

No late entries. Confirmed teams receive their track brief and problem statement drop.

Phase 03 — Inauguration

Suit Up

Opening ceremony, track briefing, and the official start of the 36-hour build window.

Phase 04 — Build Window

The City Never Sleeps

Mentor check-ins, midnight snacks, and the kind of focus that only a deadline can summon.

Phase 05 — Judging

Case Review

Teams present their builds to the judging panel across both tracks.

Phase 06 — Results

Unmasking

Winners announced, badges awarded, and the ₹25,000 prize pool distributed across tracks.
